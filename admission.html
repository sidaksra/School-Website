Admission Information

Admission Procedure

Parents seeking admission for their wards should obtain an application form for registration and prospectus from the school office.

The School is open for admission to boys and girls irrespective of caste, creed, and social status. Registration for nursery class (3+) starts in February each year. Admission to other classes is allowed subject to vacancies in each class after the declaration of the annual result.

For admission to all classes (except Nursery), a test is given to the child to assess their standard and ability. The time, date, and content of the syllabus regarding the written test/interaction will be intimated at reception during registration. Documents required at the time of admission:

  • Birth Certificate
  • S.L.C & Report Card
  • Aadhar Card of Child
  • Aadhar Card of Parents
  • Three latest passport size snaps

Note to Parents

  • The Principal reserves the right to deny admission to any candidate without assigning any reason whatsoever.
  • Fee once paid will not be refundable.
  • Tuition fee is payable for the whole academic year i.e. 12 months from April to March. Transport charges are to be paid for 11 months excluding the month of June.
  • In case of the Annual Fee payment plan, all the charges for the whole year are to be deposited in one go. In such a case, a discount of one month's tuition fee is given.
  • Annual fee payment plan can be opted only at the beginning of the session in April.
  • The parents have the choice to deposit the fee by cash or cheque.
  • In case of bouncing of the cheque due to any reason, a fine of Rs. 400/- per cheque will have to be paid by the parent.
  • All dues of the student should be cleared before each term examination otherwise he/she will not be allowed to appear in exams.
  • In case of non-payment of dues till the end of the due month, the name of the child will be struck off the rolls. The child, however, can be re-admitted with the permission of the principal. In such a case, an amount of Rs. 500/- along with up-to-date fine as per rules will be charged for re-admission.
  • School leaving certificate will be issued one week after the submission of the application for the same in the school office.
