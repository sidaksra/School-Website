Director's Message

Welcome to Shri Harkrishan Public School, where our vision is to cultivate a nurturing environment where every child's potential can truly blossom. As educators and mentors, we believe that every child is like a seedâ€”full of promise and possibilities. With the right blend of love, care, and guidance, we are dedicated to helping each student grow into a confident, independent learner who is well-prepared for the future.

At our school, we understand that the foundation of a child's development begins during these formative years. Our mission is to instill core values and essential skills that will support our students throughout their lives.

We are proud to offer a diverse range of opportunities for our students. Academically, we focus on subjects such as Science, General Knowledge, English Speaking, and Calligraphy through specialized clubs and programs. Beyond the classroom, we offer extracurricular activities that include yoga, karate, music, dance, and art and craft. These activities are designed to foster creativity, discipline, and a love for learning.

Over the years, the collective efforts of our dedicated staff and enthusiastic students have propelled our school to new heights. Today, Shri Harkrishan Public School stands as a respected institution in Jhansa, Kurukshetra, known for our commitment to excellence and innovation in education.

We reject rote learning and instead embrace a research-based approach to education. Each student is guided through a personalized learning journey that aligns with their unique abilities and interests, ensuring a comprehensive and fulfilling educational experience.

Our goal is not only to impart knowledge but to ignite a genuine joy for creative expression and discovery. With the support of our dedicated management, exceptional teachers, and cooperative parents, we are creating a child-centered learning environment where every student can thrive.

Thank you for being a part of our community. Together, we are shaping a brighter future for our children.

Warm regards,
G S Sra
Director, Shri Harkrishan Public School
