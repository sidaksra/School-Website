Director's Message

Every child is like a seed which has the potential to grow if it is nourished with love and care under the right guidance. Our goal is to graduate independent learners, whose mastery of basic skills and academic curiosity will serve as the bedrock of a purposeful life. Childhood is the best time to lay the foundation of the core values essential for the overall development of the future citizen of tomorrow.

Shri Harkrishan Public School is a place that cares the every student. Equal attention is given to the development of the children both academically like Science and G.K. Club, English Speaking and Calligraphy Clubs, Abacus and Vedic Math’s Club and in extra-curricular activities like different yoga, karate, music, dance, art and craft, etc. Year by year the efforts of all our staff and students have taken the school forward in terms of excellence. The school has progressed in leaps and bounds. Today we stand as one of the noted educational landmarks in the jhansa,Kurukshetra. Rote learning is discouraged and Research Based learning is the norm, where each pupil is guided through an individualized work plan in consonance with his/her potential, thereby providing a complete learning experience.

At Shri Harkrishan Public School, we are dedicated for our school objective to awaken the “joy” in Creative Expression and Knowledge and we are utterly committed for quality education and continuous process of improvement for the benefit of the students. A committed and supportive management, dedicated teachers, caring and co-operative parents blend harmoniously to create a child centric school.

Education is the passport to the future; tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.
