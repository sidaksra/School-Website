History

Shri Harkrishan Public School is a premier education institution of the region run by Nirmal Education Society, Kurukshetra from the year 2002 it has carved a niche in the field of education. The school is recognized from Nursery to senior secondary level and affiliated to C.B.S.E, New Delhi. It is a co-educational English Medium School situated on Jhansa-Kurukshetra Road adjoining Radha Swami Satsang Bhawan, Jhansa. The campus is spread over more than 4 acres of lush green land in a calm pleasant and pollution-free environment. The school is equipped with ultra-modern teaching and grooming facilities.

The school is named in honor of Shri Guru Harkrishan Ji, the eighth Sikh Guru, who is revered for his wisdom, compassion, and selfless service to humanity. Guru Harkrishan Ji's life and teachings are a source of inspiration for the school community. He embodied values such as humility, kindness, and the spirit of service. These values are deeply ingrained in the school's ethos and form the foundation of its educational philosophy.
