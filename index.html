About School

Shri Harkrishan Public School, located in VPO Jhansa, Kurukshetra, is renowned for its prestigious reputation in education. Our campus features modern facilities including a Pre-Primary Section, Primary, Secondary, and Senior Secondary Blocks with spacious classrooms and smart boards. We also offer a dedicated Science Block, Computer Room, Library, Home Science Room, and Activity Room. Additional amenities include a Conference Room, Fine Art Room, Music Room, Dance Room, School Canteen, Roller Skating Hall, and a Medical Room. Our expansive grounds support a variety of sports, including Badminton, Roller Skating, Basketball, and Volleyball.
