SHPS Facilities

English Language Lab

The English Language Lab is designed to enhance students' language skills through interactive and immersive learning experiences. The lab is equipped with modern audio-visual equipment and language software that aid in improving pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar. Students engage in listening, speaking, reading, and writing activities that build their confidence and proficiency in English.

In the language lab, students have access to various multimedia resources, including language games, audio books, and videos, which make learning enjoyable and effective. Our dedicated instructors provide personalized attention and guidance, ensuring that each student progresses at their own pace. The lab also prepares students for language proficiency exams and helps them develop effective communication skills for their academic and professional future.

Robotics Lab

The Robotics Lab provides students with hands-on experience in designing, building, and programming robots. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology, the lab encourages creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Students work on various projects that involve mechanical design, electronics, and software programming, gaining practical skills in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields.

Through collaborative projects and competitions, students learn to work as a team and develop leadership skills. The Robotics Lab also hosts workshops and seminars conducted by experts, giving students insights into the latest advancements in robotics and automation. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of innovators and engineers, providing them with the knowledge and tools to excel in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Sports Facility

Students participated in various Sports & Co-curricular Activities at State, District level and Won 1st and 2nd position. Students won various prizes in Poster Making, Athletics, Skating, Volleyball, Basketball, Kho-kho, Chess, Rangoli, Art & Craft, Quizzes, Dance, Music etc. Our students show remarkable performance in Scholastic & Co-scholastic areas time to time and bring laurels to the school.

The institution has grounds for Basketball, Skating, Volleyball, Kho-Kho, Athletics etc. School imparts training to budding sports persons in various sports disciplines like athletics, Kho-Kho volleyball etc. Inter House competitions are organized for various games and a sports meet is held every year. In primary wing also the sports competitions are organized time to time. School has a Skating rink where training of skating and Taekwondo is given by trained coaches. Students participate in games up to District, State, and National levels.

Transport Facility

Our school provides a reliable and efficient transport facility with its own fleet of buses, ensuring convenient and safe travel for students. These buses cover almost all directions in the town and neighboring areas, making it accessible for students from various locations. Each bus follows fixed routes with designated stops, ensuring timely and organized pick-ups and drop-offs.

The safety of children during travel is our top priority. Our buses are operated by trained and experienced drivers who are well-versed in safe driving practices. Additionally, there are personnel on board each bus to supervise the students and ensure their well-being throughout the journey.

Science Labs

Our school boasts modern and well-equipped labs for chemistry, physics, and mathematics, providing students with excellent facilities for conducting experiments and practical work. In the chemistry lab, students can explore various chemical reactions and learn about different compounds. The physics lab allows students to understand fundamental principles through experiments with light, electricity, and mechanics. The math lab helps students visualize complex concepts through interactive tools and models.

We encourage students to create models and engage in hands-on learning, fostering a deeper understanding of scientific principles. Our dedicated faculty members guide students in their projects and experiments, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

Audio Visual Classes

Children learn best through sensory experiences, and our school leverages this by incorporating a variety of audio-visual aids in classroom instruction. These aids include pictures, slides, radios, videos, and other multimedia tools that enhance the learning experience. By engaging multiple senses, these tools help students better understand and retain information, making lessons more interactive and enjoyable.

To further support student learning, we provide access to a comprehensive learning app. This app allows students to monitor their grades, track their progress, and access eBooks and other educational resources. The app also includes various activities and exercises to reinforce classroom learning and provide additional practice. Through this app, students can stay organized and take control of their educational journey, ensuring they have all the resources they need at their fingertips.
